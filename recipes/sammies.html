Loaded Cucumber Sammies

Description

Cucumber sandwiches are always so good but so small. So, lets make it loaded.

Ingredients

  • 2 Thick slices of Multi-grain bread
  • 2 tbsp Cream Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Alfalfa sprouts
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 6 slices Cucumber
  • 1 Leaf Lettuce
  • 1 oz Pepperoncini
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Spread 1 tbsp cream cheese on each slice of bread.
  2. On one slice spread out the cucumber slices in a single layer.
  3. Cover the cucumber with the sprouts, then sprinkle with oil and vinegar.
  4. Layer on the tomato slices, lettuce, and pepperoncini.
  5. Spread other slice of bread with the mashed avocado.
  6. Finally close the sammie and enjoy!
