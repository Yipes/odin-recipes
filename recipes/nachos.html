Hot Cheeto Nachos

Description

Nachos but better and microwavable. Feel free to change or add any toppings you'd like or prefer instead of what is listed. This recipe will be quick, easy, and most definetly not that healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 microwave safe bowl or plate
  • 1 bag of Flaming Hot Cheetos
  • Shredded cheese of your choice
  • Fresh or pickled jalapeño slices

Steps

  1. Pour a good layer of cheetos onto plate
  2. Add on a handful of cheese
  3. Microwave until cheese melts
  4. Top with jalapeños
  5. Enjoy
